The problem.

People often view Africa as being similar, though the differences in different regions are extreme. According to UNDP Africa is often viewed as lacking educated workers, low infrastructures and weak judicial systems.

1. This scares investor and companies from investing in the continent.

2. Africas reputation has been misrepresented for centuries, which would in turn hinder their growth globally.

African entrepreneurs face some of the world's thoughest business conditions. The biggest challenge they face is risk-averse banks and lack of capital. Studies show that Sub-Saharan companies are 24% smaller than similar businesses accros the globe.

Africa was the mos profitable continent for investors between 2006 and 2011, Africa has vast natural resources, combined with a young and increasingly educated population. The continent as a whole, faces one of the best possibilities for growth in the world.
