Create an account

Entrepreneurs create accounts where they can manage different projects. Explain who you are and what you stand for, and in which areas you wish to innovate.

Create a project

Fill out the neccessary information about your project and what resources you are looking for: capital, graphic design, etc. Each project is tagged by which types of resources are needed, and matched by what contributors and provide.

3

Once you register your project, relevant contributors will contact you and offer their services. Filter based on resources offered, respond directly in our chat, and start collaborating. Our knowledgeable support-staff is ready to help you at any point througout the process.
