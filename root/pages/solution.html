Our solution.

Problems.

•

People are not investing in the African continent beacause of outdated beliefs, they do not know where to invest or they are discouraged by language and cultural barriers. These causes disbelief in Africa as a suitable investment opportunity, causing the continent to take tolls in economic growth.

•

Charity work is often the main lens in which people view Africa. This creates a stereotype of Africans as all being poor and in need of assistance. Again and again Africans are perceived by the world as being unable to handle themselves without the help of other developed countries.

•

Banking services that are lacking proficiency, are not making sure payments arrive correctly and safely, thus it can be tricky for entrepreneurs to grow their businesses. African entrepreneurs can also be vulnerable to exploitation by foreign companies that view them as a easy business targets.

What can be done.

•

Give exposure to African entrepreneurs so they can get the funding and resources they need, by making it easy for investors and contributors from other continents to get in touch and bridge cultural and language barriers.

•

Educating people on false misconceptions about Africa to raise the entrepreneural spirit, legitimizing ideas to the same level as other startups, and making people trust the beneficial entrepreneural situation Africa is in.

•

Hiring competent people with expertise in technology security to assist African entrepreneurs by making it possible to offer safe and trackable payments while also providing assistance and guidance to them.

Problems.

•

An all-in-one platform that gives exposure to the most innovative projects in Africa, so they can gain the funding and resources needed to reach their goals.

•

Creating a service that does not underestimate African entrepreneurship, and communicates to investors and contributors the real value of projects.

•

A safe and trackable payment service, and staffing African/English speakers locally who can help with everything from translation, negotiation and security.
