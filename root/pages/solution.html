Our solution.

Problems.

•

Many are not investing in the African continent due to outdated beliefs, not knowing where to invest, or because they are discouraged by language and cultural barriers. These factors scare away potential investors.

•

Charity and relief work are what most people think of when you say Africa. Although it is true in many cases, it creates a harmful stereotype that all Africans are poor and in need.

•

Many African countries lack safe and secure banking, which are fundamental for businesses to survive and thrive. African entrepreneurs are, for this reason, vulnerable to exploitation by foreign companies and states.

What can be done.

•

A large barrier for African entrepreneurs to get funding is the culture and language barrier for businesses and western investors. By training local staff to help these businesses, we can minimize these barriers.

•

Educate people on misconceptions about Africa. Africa was the most profitable continent from 2006 to 2011. By showcasing successful African businesses, we can overcome these misconceptions.

•

Safe and secure banking is a necessity for businesses and entrepreneurs. By making local financial services better, we can enable entrepreneurs to succeed.

Our solution.

•

An all-in-one platform that gives exposure to the most innovative projects in Africa. Contributors can discover, meet, and invest in exciting projects.

•

Contributors can provide money, services, or other resources that entrepreneurs are looking for. We want to enable anyone willing to make a positive impact.

•

A safe and trackable payment service, and local and english speaking speaking expert staff. Our staff can help with everything from translation, negotiation to security.
